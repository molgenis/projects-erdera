MOLGENIS-RD3

The Rare Disease Database

Overview

MOLGENIS-RD3 is a model and database that provides the structure for capturing and managing research metadata. This includes characteristics about individuals and samples that were collected from them, as well as metadata on the experiments performed and the results that were produced (e.g., files variant interpretations, etc.).
